**Account Owner or Administrators:** Before adding [Campfire](http://campfirenow.com/ "Link opens in a new window") notifications to New Relic, you must have an existing Campfire account with the following information:

* Subdomain
* Email
* Authentication token
* Campfire room names as applicable

## Contents [#qiklinks]

## Creating a Campfire channel [#channel]

![screen alert channel campfire.png](./images/screen-alert-channel-campfire_0.png "screen alert channel campfire.png")

<figcaption>
  **Alerts > Alert policies > Channels and groups:** Your New Relic account's Owner or Admins can integrate your Campfire account as an alert notification channel.
</figcaption>

To integrate your Campfire account with New Relic:

1. From the New Relic menu bar, select **Alerts > Alert policies > Channels and groups**.
2. From the Channels section, select **Create channel > Campfire**.
3. Provide the information for your Campfire account.
4. Select your [notification level](/docs/alerts/alert-policies/configuring-alerts/alert-channels-maintaining-notification-lists#levels).
5. Select **Integrate with Campfire**.

**Tip:** Fields have tooltip links to help you retrieve information from Campfire.
